## General Questions

### What is StartIntel?

StartIntel is an intelligent platform that helps startup founders and entrepreneurs gather, analyze, and act on market intelligence. It automates competitive analysis, market research, and investor tracking.

### Who should use StartIntel?

- **Founders** building new startups
- **Product Managers** tracking competitors
- **Investors** researching market opportunities
- **Marketing Teams** analyzing market trends
- **Business Analysts** gathering competitive intelligence

### Is StartIntel free?

We offer a **Free Tier** with limited analyses. Premium features require a paid plan.

### What data sources does StartIntel use?

- News websites
- Social media platforms
- Company websites
- Industry reports
- Job boards
- Patent databases
- Government records
- Public financial data

## Getting Started

### How do I sign up?

1. Visit the website
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Verify your email
5. You're ready to go!

### How long does the first analysis take?

**First Analysis**: 2-5 minutes
**Subsequent Analyses**: Usually 1-3 minutes
**Real-time Monitoring**: Instant (for premium users)

### Do I need technical knowledge to use StartIntel?

No! StartIntel is designed for non-technical users. However, API integration requires basic technical knowledge.

### Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel anytime without penalties. Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation.

## Features & Functionality

### What types of analyses can I run?

- **Competitive Analysis** - Analyze direct competitors
- **Market Trends** - Identify industry trends
- **Investor Intelligence** - Track funding landscape
- **Product Analysis** - Compare product features

Yes, we can analyze private companies based on available public information. Accuracy may vary depending on data availability.

### How often is data updated?

- **Enterprise**: Real-time (continuous)

### Can I export reports?

Yes! Export formats include:
- PDF (professional reports)

## Team & Collaboration

### Can I invite team members?

Yes! All paid plans support team members:
- **Pro Plan**: Up to 10 members
- **Enterprise**: Unlimited members

### How do I share an analysis?

1. Click the "Share" button
2. Enter team member emails
3. Set permission level
4. Click "Send Invite"

### What permission levels exist?

- **Viewer**: Read-only access
- **Editor**: Can modify analyses
- **Admin**: Full access including settings

### Can team members edit my analyses?

Yes, if you grant them "Editor" or "Admin" permissions. You can revoke access anytime.

## Troubleshooting

### Why is my analysis failing?

Check if:
- Company name is spelled correctly
- You have internet connection
- Your API quota isn't exceeded
- See [[Troubleshooting]] for more

### What if my data looks wrong?

1. Try refreshing the analysis
2. Check data sources are enabled
3. Contact support if issue persists

### Can I pause my subscription?

Yes! You can pause for up to 3 months. Your data is preserved.

### How do I reset my password?

1. Click "Forgot Password" on login
2. Enter your email
3. Click link in reset email
4. Create new password
